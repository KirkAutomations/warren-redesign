About Judge Warren

A Lifetime Dedicated to Justice & the Constitution

Judge Michael Warren has served on the Oakland County Circuit Court for over two decades, presiding over more than 360 jury trials spanning over 1,000 days. His record of trial experience surpasses that of the entire United States Supreme Court combined.

A cum laude graduate of the University of Michigan Law School and holder of an Honors History Degree from Wayne State University, Judge Warren brings both legal expertise and deep historical perspective to the bench. He interprets the law as written — never legislating from the bench.
