About the Judge

A Lifetime of Service to Justice

Judge Michael Warren has served on the Oakland County Circuit Court for over two decades, presiding over more than 360 jury trials and earning recognition as Judge of the Decade by the International Association of Top Professionals.

A graduate of the University of Michigan Law School (cum laude) and Wayne State University (Honors History), Judge Warren combines deep legal scholarship with an unwavering commitment to constitutional principles and equal justice under the law.

Beyond the bench, he is a constitutional law professor at WMU Cooley Law School, the author of "America's Survival Guide," and co-founder of Patriot Week — a national celebration of American first principles.
